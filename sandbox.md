
#### KAAR presentations content
##### Plan to produce
1. Title card 
2. Definition and describe the plan to produce
3. How it is important
4. Flow chart the business process
5. Stakeholders and departments involved
6. Illustrate how SAP Modules (Example: SD, MM, PP, FI, HCM) enable or automate parts of this process.
7. Highlight pain points or inefficiencies in the manual process.
8. Mention the expected business outcomes of using SAP for that process


 Ensures that the right product is manufactured at the right time, using optimal resources and materials.
